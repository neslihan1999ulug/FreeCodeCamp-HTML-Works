Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake is rich, moist, and easy to make for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup cocoa powder
  • 2 eggs

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. Add the eggs and mix until smooth.
  4. Pour into a baking pan and bake for 30 minutes.
